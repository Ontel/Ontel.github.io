Uplink™ streamlines the deployment and implementation process throughout all stages of the project's workflow from Survey, BOM Creation, Scoping, Check-in/out, Construction, Quality Assurance, and Closeout. Built for mobile teams and remote-management resources, Uplink™ helps to move your team away from Excel and into specialized and user friendly applications that fit your exact needs.
ScoreCard™ allows project-management teams to quickly compile project data and KPI frameworks into a single, customizable tool. With ScoreCard™, managers can drill into the performance of projects and contractors to instantly understand trends from real-time performance data. GC's and markets can know their relative ranking and begin to understand how they fit in the pack.
SweepAnalytics™ automates the labelling, marker positioning, and processing of Sweep results. It then validates the test results to ensure they are complete and meet your performance requirements. Sweep processing and validation time is cut down to minutes instead of hours.

Services

  • Closeout and Testing

    From us testing on site or remotely to photography, technical review, automation, and QA, our closeout services help deliver a COP that meets the customer's spec faster than anyone else.

  • Software

    We design and adapt software specifically for wireless construction companies. We have developed tailored software such as SweepAnalytics™ for Automated Testing Validation, ScoreCard™ for Performance Analysis, and Uplink™, our Wireless Construction mobile application.

  • Hardware

    Our ComPacks™ connect you with your team in a streamlined and simple manner. Our integrate hardware solutions put us in the field with your crews to support and closeout in realtime.

  • Reporting and Coordination

    With our team, your COP services are augmented by live staff that can help them bridge the gap between reporting and getting the work done, so that you can get the information into and out of the field necessary to get the job done right.

  • Training and Support

    Training and support to get the job done right, but most importantly the first time. Our training services are designed to be immersive and efficient, helping your team cost effectively get the training they need throughout the project.

  • Metrics

    Measuring performance is key to improving it. Ontel puts the right information where you need it, allowing your team to instantly access KPI's, without needing anything more than a phone.
