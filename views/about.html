About Us
Founded in 2013, Ontel is a Washington D.C. based company that provides wireless infrastructure services companies business and project management software, as well as specialized technical services, management, and administration.
Our mission: To empower the world with technology
Helping to build networks that connect people is our passion. We are proud to provide software and services to help businesses and communities build the next-generation networks that help people stay in contact, live better, and create opportunities.
