About Us
Founded in 2013, Ontel is a Washington D.C. based software and management services company catering to telecommunication contractors, engineering firms, and project management companies. Our team consists of customer service oriented technicians, managers, and software developers with a breadth of skills and experiences across all areas of site development and implementation. Prior to Ontel, we led teams on behalf of the largest carriers and engineering firms to successfully deliver the latest telecommunication technologies across the U.S.
Our mission: To empower the world with technology
Helping to build networks that connect people is our passion. We are proud to provide software and services to help businesses and communities build the next-generation networks that help people communicate, collaborate, and live better.
