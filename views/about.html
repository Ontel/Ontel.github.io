About Us
Founded in August of 2013, Ontel is a Washington D.C. based LLC that provides software and management services to telecommunication contractors, engineering firms, real estate specialists, and project management companies. Our team consists of engineers, designers, technicians, and others that all collaborate to help our clients close projects better. To us, closing a project in the wireless industry is a reflection of the organization and how professionally it has operated as a whole, as such, we look at all of the aspects associated with closing well, like how people in the organization use tech, how is sharing done, who is technical etc. From there we leverage our resources and experience to help clients realize the next level.
Our mission: To empower the world with technology
Helping to build networks that connect people with the world is our passion. We are proud to provide software and services to help businesses and communities build the next generation of experiences which inspire people to share, build businesses, and live happier.
