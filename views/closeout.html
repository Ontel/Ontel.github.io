How it Works
Share Client Requirements and Specifications
  • Tell us about your project and share your project specification documents
  • Provide an example C.O.P. that the customer likes, or we can help to create a gold standard if needed
Setup the Systems
  • An environment with your goals, requirements, and processes is then set up in Swift Projects by us
  • Depending on your deliverables, we may then start creating custom software applications to automate the processing and validation of key data deliverables like RETS and test results etc.
Execute the Plan
  • We receive site information and scheduling in Swift and ensure any SOW specific items are identified in the tasks
  • We review site information for discrepancies for items like RF-related requirements for example
  • We receive and review your raw files in Swift and advise on their accuracy and completeness
  • We compile and validate C.O.P., complete necessary documents, and upload to client portal
  • When rejections occur, we document them and incorporate into Swift Projects to prevent them from occuring in the future
Optimize Performance
  • We analyze performance by looking at key metrics such as how long it takes to receive raw data from the field, time taken to compile, QA issues, etc
  • We then present our findings and solutions and come up with a plan of action to optimize performance
  • This process is repeated at set intervals based on work volume and rates of KPI failures
