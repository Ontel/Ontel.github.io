How it Works
Share Client Requirements and Specifications
  • Tell us about your project and share your project specification documents.
  • Provide an example C.O.P., or we can help to create a gold standard if needed.
Setup the Systems
  • An environment with your goals, requirements, and processes is then set up in Swift Projects™ by us.
  • We will then build profiles for your projects throughout our suite of automation tools for their review and validation.
Execute the Plan
  • We receive site information and scheduling in Swift Projects™ and ensure any SOW specific items are identified in the tasks.
  • We review site information for discrepancies related with our identification of C.O.P. specific specifications and requirements.
  • We receive and review your raw files and advise on their accuracy and completeness.
  • We compile and validate C.O.P. checking for, and notating discrepancies, while completing necessary documents, and uploading to client portal for review and acceptance upon completion.
Optimize Performance
  • After gathering sufficient data, we analyze performance and KPI's.
  • We then present our findings and solutions and come up with a plan of action to optimize performance.
  • This process is repeated at set intervals based on work volume and rates of KPI failures.
