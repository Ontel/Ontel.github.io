How it Works
Share Client Requirements and Specifications
  • Tell us about your project and exchange specification documents which we then distill into a simple, comprehensive market requirement summary
  • We seek out an example of a C.O.P. that the client likes or create a gold standard if needed
Setup the Systems
  • An environment in Swift Projects is set up and people are invited and oriented to it
  • A project is setup in Swift Traces with project specifications programmed to automate sweep processing, validation, and results output
Execute the Plan
  • We recieve site information and scheduling
  • We review SOW documents for completeness and advise accordingly
  • We provide technical and quality assurance of tests and photos
  • We close the site within your time requirements
Optimize Performance
  • We analyze performance from data generated from punch items and customer feedback, and with this data adapt systems and people to meet expectations
Common C.O.P. Deliverables
We verify the accuracy of the deliverables for each of the documents below as applicable to the SOW. We verify whenever possible against as-built data (photos, tests, etc) or the applicable spec document.
  • QA for tests and photos
  • Redline assistance for drawings and RF design
  • Carrier and LL specific forms
  • Punch-item corrections
  • Height Verification Reports
  • Antenna Alignment Tool reports
  • RET Screenshots and worksheet
  • Certifications for equipment and people
  • Jobsite safety documents (JSA/JHA)
  • Log Book entries
  • Site clear confirmations
  • Material releases and bills of lading
