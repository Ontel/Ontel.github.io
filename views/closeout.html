How it Works
Share Client Requirements and Specifications
  • Tell us about your project and share your project specification documents
  • We seek out an example C.O.P. that the client likes or we can help to create a gold standard if needed
Setup the Systems
  • An environment with your goals, requirements, and processes is then setup in Swift Projects based on your requirements
  • Depending on your deliverables, we may then start creating custom software applications to automate the processing and validation of key data deliverables like RETS and Tests
Execute the Plan
  • We recieve site information and scheduling and ensure any SOW specific items are identified in Swift Projects
  • We receive and review your raw files and advise on their accuracy and completeness
  • We compile C.O.P, complete necessary documents, and upload to client portal
  • If rejections occur, we document them and incorporate it into Swift Projects to prevent them from occuring in the future
Optimize Performance
  • We analyze performance by looking at key metrics such as how long it takes to receive raw data from the field, time taken to compile, QA, etc
  • We then present our findings and solutions and come up with a plan of action to optimize performance
  • This process is repeated at set intervals based on work volume and project timelines
Common C.O.P. Deliverables
We check all deliverables for completeness and accuracy. We verify whenever possible against as-built data (photos, tests, etc) or the applicable spec document.
  • Quality assurance for tests and photos
  • Redline assistance for drawings and RF design as-built's
  • Carrier and LL specific forms related with the SOW
  • Punch-item correction tracking and submittal
  • Height Verification reports
  • Antenna Alignment Tool reports
  • RET Screenshots and worksheets
  • Certifications for equipment and people
  • Jobsite safety documents (JSA/JHA)
  • Log Book entries
  • Site clear confirmations
  • Material releases and bills of lading
