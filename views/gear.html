Gear
OnPacks™ are heavy-duty Pelican backpacks filled with the items your teams need to close out in real-time.
What's inside?
OnPacks are composed of the following:
  • Large screen current model smartphone
  • Touch screen compact laptop loaded with remote access software for back-office login and support of closeout
  • Router and Modem for LTE connectivity and test unit networking
  • Portable battery supply
  • USB Sticks
  • SD Cards
How it Works
1. Tell us how many you need and when
2. We handle the cellular and data plans
3. We replace the contents every 2 years or as necessary. You handle replacement cost at the value of the item at the time of damage (saves customers money).
Why do we do it?
We perform the gear outfitting simply because we know how much of a hassle finding, maintaining, and managing phones and computers can be. We’ve tried to provide access to this equipment at near cost because we believe giving our clients access to high quality hardware is critical to their success and ours.
