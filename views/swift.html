Swift Projects
A Mobile-Centric Project Management Platform
Make How You Think Into a System
Swift Projects puts the power to design, run, and close wireless projects into the palm of your hand. With a phone or browser, Swift enables you to design detailed project workflows with 3 simple building blocks. Those blocks then automate administrative and management tasks like status updates, scheduling, uploading and downloading, and relabelling files automatically. Most importantly for our clients, Swift Projects eliminates mistakes and waste related with sharing and collaborating ineffectively.
One of the main ways we do this is to make it simple to take photos and videos from the field and put them directly into the QA approval process live. There's so much more that we've packed into it, but at the end of the day, it's simple enough to set up and customize in a couple of hours a system that could completely change the way you think about running your projects and managing your resources. Give it a try, we'd be happy to assist with you setting up and running a pilot project within your organization to see if it's the right fit for your team.
